The Ram Mandir

A sunny day in Tours

The Ram Mandir in Ayodhya is a three-story temple dedicated to Lord Rama, believed to be his birthplace. It is being constructed in the traditional Nagara style, with intricate carvings and symbolic representations. The temple complex is spread over 2.7 acres, including a built-up area of 57,400 square feet. It features 392 pillars, 44 doors, and 5 Mandapas (halls). The temple's idols are crafted from 60-million-year-old Shaligram stones from the Gandaki River in Nepa
